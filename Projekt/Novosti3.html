Red v blue, Emery v Sarri... Arsenal v Chelsea

Another week, another London derby as Chelsea cross the capital for a blockbuster encounter against Unai Emery's men at Emirates Stadium.

We faced the Blues twice at home last season, drawing 2-2 in the Premier League at the start of January 2018 before beating them 2-1 in the EFL Cup semi-final second leg three weeks later.

This time around we’re looking to make up ground on Maurizio Sarri's side, who are six points ahead of us going into Saturday’s fixture.

Aubameyan has 14 goals this seasone, while Hazard 10.

Chelsea are 6 points ahed Arsenal.

This is going to be very interesting game tomorow.
